Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Exser Books handles information.

1. Information Collection

We do not require users to create an account to browse or download books. We only collect data through Google Authentication for administrative access (owner only).

2. Cookies and Analytics

We may use basic cookies to ensure the website functions correctly and to save your preferences. We do not track personal identification information.

3. Third-Party Links

Exser Books provides links to external PDF files (e.g., Google Drive). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

4. Rating System

When you rate a book, your vote is stored anonymously in our database to calculate the global average score.
